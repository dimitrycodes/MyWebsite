You can create your car list, store their metrics, and update them as new cars are put on the market or if you made an error.

Spaced Repetition Learning

Space repetition learning enables you to learn a new language and retain the information at a higher rate. To begin the person learning starts with a new word and the more often that the word is correctly guessed, the less they see that word and if they happen to guess a word incorrectly then the more often they will see the word to help them master it.

Goats

Goats is an app that enables you to store your top players in a sport according to 6 key statistics. Those statistics will help you make the case for which player is the greatest player of all time. You can create your list, store their statistics, and update them if the player is still playing or if you decide that the player doesn't belong in the list because another player is better.
